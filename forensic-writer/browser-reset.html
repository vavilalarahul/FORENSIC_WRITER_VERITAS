🔄 Forensic Writer - Browser Reset

Follow these steps to completely reset your browser cache and force the login page to load.

Step 1
Clear Browser Storage

Open browser console (F12) and run:

localStorage.clear();
sessionStorage.clear();
Step 2
Hard Refresh Browser

Press Ctrl + Shift + R (or Cmd + Shift + R on Mac)

This will force a complete reload and clear cached data.

Step 3
Clear Cookies

In browser console:

document.cookie.split(";").forEach(function(c) { document.cookie = c.replace(/^ +/, "").replace(/=.*/, "=;expires=Thu, 01 Jan 1970 00:00:00 GMT;path=/"); });
Step 4
Go to Application

Navigate to: http://localhost:5173

You should now see the login page.

Step 5

Click "Test Complete" when finished.
